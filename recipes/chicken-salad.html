Chicken Salad

Description

Delicious and easy to make chicken salad for big gains

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped chicken
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 rib celery chopped
  • 1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place almonds in frying pan and toast over medium-high heat
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in bowl
  3. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery
  4. Eat
